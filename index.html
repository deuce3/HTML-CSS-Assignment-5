Weight Training Guide

Bigger Biceps

Want bigger arms? It starts with dumbbells. Get started lifting slow and light and progress to slow and heavy. Our gyms offer dumbbells ranging from 2lbs to 200lbs. There are not too many gyms in the world open to the public that offer the range of dumbbell weights that our gyms offer.

Stronger Core

Did you know one of the best ways to build a strong, toned core is by boxing? Our gym offers a private punching bag as well as boxing classes. If you are looking for an activity outside of weight lifting and running to build endurance and your dream core boxing may be for you.
